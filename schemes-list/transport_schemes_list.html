Ministry Of Housing & Urban Affairs

Atal Mission For Rejuvenation And Urban Transformation

Providing basic services (e.g. water supply, sewerage, urban transport) to households and building amenities in cities which will improve the quality of life for all, especially the poor and the disadvantaged is a national priority.


Ministry Of New and Renewable Energy

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Suraksha Evam Utthan Mahabhiyan Scheme For Farmers.

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Urja Surakshaevam Utthaan Mahabhiyan Yojana (PM-KUSUM Scheme) was launched in March 2019 by the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE), to subsidize farmers to install solar irrigation pumps for cultivation. Each farmer will receive a 60% subsidy.


Ministry Of New and Renewable Energy

Phase-II Of The Grid Connected Rooftop Solar Programme

Phase-II of the Grid Connected Rooftop Solar Programme, this programme is introduced by Ministry of New & Renewable Energy(MNRE), GoI with JAKEDA as a nodal agency in the UT of J&K for achieving cumulative capacity of 220MW under 20MW Residential Scheme and 200 MW Jammu Solar City “Solar City.


Ministry of Education

AICTE - Grant For Augmenting Infrastructure In North Eastern Region

The scheme "AICTE - Grant for Augmenting Infrastructure in North Eastern Region (GAINER)" was introduced by AICTE to enhance the functional efficiency of the technical institutes located in far-flung areas of North East India, by addressing deficiencies in water, electricity, and internet.


Ministry Of Rural Development

Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana

With the objective of providing rural connectivity, the Government of India launched the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana (referred to as PMGSY-I hereinafter) on 25th December 2000 to provide all-weather access to eligible unconnected habitations.
