Sports & Culture


Ministry Of Youth Affairs & Sports

Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay National Welfare Fund For Sportspersons

A welfare scheme by MYAS providing suitable assistance to the meritorious and outstanding sportspersons of yesteryear who had brought glory to the country in international field who are now living in indigent circumstances.


Ministry Of Youth Affairs & Sports

Free Education For Sports Medal Winners / Participants Of National/ International Events

The scheme of "Free Education for Sports Medal Winners / Participants of National/ International Events" is introduced by UGC, to provide financial assistance to the medal winners\ elite sports persons studying in universities/colleges leading to all the full-time regular courses.

Ministry Of Culture

Scheme For Pension And Medical Aid To Artistes

A pension scheme by MoC improving the financial and socio-economic status of the old artists and scholars who have contributed significantly in their specialized fields, but due to old age they have to lead a miserable life or are in penury condition.


Ministry Of Youth Affairs & Sports

Dhyan Chand Award For Life-time Achievement Sports And Games

Named after the hockey wizard Major Dhyan Chand (1905–79), an Indian legendary field hockey player, "Dhyan Chand Award for Life-time Achievement Sports and Games" is awarded annually by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports.


Ministry Of Youth Affairs & Sports

Tenzing Norgay National Adventure Award

MYAS confers the Tenzing Norgay National Adventure Award in order to recognize the achievements of persons associated with the field of adventure and to encourage young people to develop the spirit of endurance, risk-taking, cooperative teamwork, and quick, ready, and effective reflexes.

Ministry Of Youth Affairs & Sports

Maulana Abul Kalam Azad Trophy Scheme

The MAKA Trophy instituted in year 1956-57 is a rolling trophy awarded by the Ministry of Sports to the overall top performing University in sports of the preceding year for promoting competitive sports in the university sector with a view to arouse keen interest among students.


Ministry Of Youth Affairs & Sports

Scheme Of Sports Fund For Pension To Meritorious Sportspersons

retirement from active sports career in the form of a lifetime pension, payable on attaining the age of 30 years (or date of retirement from active sports whichever is later).

Ministry Of Youth Affairs & Sports

Scheme For Dronacharya Award For Outstanding Coaches In Sports And Games

The Dronacharya Award for Outstanding Coaches in Sports and Games is intended to honor coaches who have done outstanding and meritorious work on a consistent basis and enabled sportspersons to excel in international events.
