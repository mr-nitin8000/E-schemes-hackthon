Skills & Employment


Ministry Of Rural Development

Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act

An employment scheme by the MoRD, providing provide at least 100 days of guaranteed wage employment in a financial year to every rural household whose adult members volunteer to do unskilled work. Any Indian citizen above the age of 18 years and residing in a rural area can apply to this scheme.

Ministry Of Rural Development

Deen Dayal Upadhyay Grameen Kaushalya Yojana

A skill development scheme by Ministry of Rural Development for for poor rural youth, and for women, vulnerable tribes, PWDs, transgender and special groups like bonded labours and trafficking victims.


Ministry Of Science And Technology

Women Scientist Scheme-B

A Research Grant by MoST for female candidates with break in their career interested in addressing societal challenges through viable S&T interventions. Women scientists who wish to apply under this scheme are required to develop their own project/proposals for scientific and technological solutions


Ministry Of Woman and Child Development

SWADHAR Greh (A Scheme For Women In Difficult Circumstances)

Swadhar Greh is a scheme by Ministry of Women and Child Development intended that caters to primary needs of women in difficult circumstances.


Ministry Of Youth Affairs & Sports

National Youth Corps

A volunteership scheme by Ministry of Youth Affairs And Sports to tap the potential of the disciplined and dedicated youth In the age group of 18 to 29 years who have the inclination and spirit to engage in nation building exercises, to serve up to two years in Nation Building activities.

Jammu and Kashmir

HIMAYAT- Deen Dayal Upadhyaya-Grameen Kaushalya Yojana

HIMAYAT is a placement-linked placement-linked market-driven skill training program by the Ministry of Rural Development for the unemployed youth of J&K. The scheme aims at providing entrepreneurial skills for sustainable livelihood to 30,000 youth of J&K and facilitating access to finance.

Ministry Of Skill Development And Entrepreneurship

Skill Loan Scheme

A Skill loan scheme by Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship to provide loan upto Rs. 1,50,000 to student who has secured admission in a course run by Industrial Training Institutes (ITIs), Polytechnics or in a school recognized by Central or State education Boards or in a college affil .

Ministry Of Home Affairs

Central Assistance For One Time Settlement Of Displaced Families From PoK And Chhamb Under Prime Minister's Development Package

A relief and rehabilitation scheme by the Ministry of Home Affairs provides central assistance to the displaced families from PoK And Chhamb and settled in the state of Jammu & Kashmir. The amount of Central Assistance of Rs. 5,49,692/- per family will be distributed through Direct Benefit Transfer.


Ministry Of Minority Affairs

Seekho Aur Kamao

A Skill development scheme by Ministry of Minority Affairs to provide skill development and placement assistance to Minority community


Ministry Of Tourism

Capacity Building For Service Providers - Entrepreneurship Programme

The scheme "Entrepreneurship Programme" is a Suo-Moto Sub-Scheme of the Ministry of Tourism under the Umbrella Scheme of "Capacity Building for Service Providers". In this scheme financial assistance is provided to the institutes (given below) to conduct entrepreneurship development programs.


Ministry Of Tourism

Capacity Building For Service Providers - Skill Testing & Certification

The scheme "Skill Testing & Certification" is a Suo-Moto Sub-Category of Programmes under the Umbrella Scheme of "Capacity Building for Service Providers", Ministry of Tourism. In this scheme, financial assistance is provided to conduct Skilling and Re-Skilling Programmes.
